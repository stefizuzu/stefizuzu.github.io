Stock market prediction using Sentiment Analysis

Project information

  • Category: NLP
  • Tech stack: Python
  • Project: Link to DiVA

Thesis written for the bachelor's degree at Royal Institute of Technology, KTH. With the help of Python language and other technologies, including technical analysis, I tried to predict the share movement depending on the subjective opinion of the people on a Swedish forum. The analysis utilizes machine learning to train three machine learning models with textdata and stockdata, models such as SVM, Naive bayes and Decision trees. Sentiment analysis is used to find subjectivity in the form of emotions (sentiment) from text.
