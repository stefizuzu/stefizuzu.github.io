Human-food interaction: Using digital tools to optimize food consumption

Project information

  • Category: Full-Stack
  • Tech stack: React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap & Express.JS

Master thesis. Scooping into the field of Human-Food Interaction (HFI). Food is undeniably an essential facet of our life. Cooking becomes just another task, one that can be controlled and contained.

The main aim of the web application is to encourage people to more domestic cooking but also use their current food inventory instead of buying new ingredients and leaving the existing products likely to reach their expiration date and go to waste.
It contains three primary elements: a server module, a client module, and a database. t has different features and components e.g. authentication system, existing recipe customization, a search function, a favorites list, a reset function, and alternative ingredient recommendations.
